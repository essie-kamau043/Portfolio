About Me

Hi! I'm Esther Wambui, a passionate Frontend Developer from Nairobi, Kenya. I specialize in building responsive, user-friendly websites using modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6+, and frameworks like React and Bootstrap.

With over a year of hands-on experience, I've developed a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of user experience principles. My journey in web development started with a curiosity for creating beautiful digital experiences. Today, I'm committed to staying at the forefront of technology trends, constantly learning new tools and techniques to deliver cutting-edge solutions that exceed client expectations.
